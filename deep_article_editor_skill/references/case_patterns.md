# Case Patterns: Deep Analysis Article Techniques

These patterns are distilled from the analyzed Token article and should be used as reusable writing moves, not copied mechanically.

## 1. Start by negating a popular but incomplete belief
Pattern: “X is often called Y, but this analogy is only partly true.”

Purpose: Create cognitive tension and make the reader want the correction.

## 2. Move from concept explanation to structural change
Weak question: “What is X?”
Strong question: “What economic, organizational, or social order does X begin to change?”

## 3. Use infrastructure analogies
Examples of analogy families:

- Token vs. kilowatt-hour: measurement unit, not currency.
- Selling resources vs. selling outcomes.
- From selling electricity to selling illumination.

Use only when the mapping is precise.

## 4. Build memorable models
Examples:

- “GPU → energy → talent” as a migrating pricing anchor.
- “Cost floor, capability ceiling.”
- “Double S-curve → J-shaped burst → sublinear convergence.”
- “Two critical thresholds: price threshold and cognition threshold.”

## 5. Add power questions
A strong industry article asks who controls standards, interfaces, pricing anchors, distribution, and surplus capture.

## 6. Elevate the conclusion
The article should end by explaining what broader transition the topic signals.

Possible forms:

- From tools to automated systems.
- From labor-driven growth to machine-driven operations.
- From adding AI to a production function to rewriting the production function itself.
